The Golden Rule By Jesus Christ

“All things, therefore, that you want men to do to you,
you also must do to them. This, in fact, is what the
Law and the Prophets mean".

The Greatest Commands as Stated by Jesus

“‘You must love Jehovah your God with your whole heart
and with your whole soul and with your whole mind.
This is the greatest and first commandment. The second,
like it, is this: ‘You must love your neighbor as yourself.
On these two commandments the whole Law hangs and the
Prophets.”



Description of Love by Apostle Paul

Building on the principles stated by Jesus Christ, the
apostle Paul explained what true love means.
In his first letter to the Christian congregation in the
city of Corinth, chapter 13 started with the statement
"If I speak in the tongues of men and of angels but do not
have love, I have become a clanging gong or a clashing cymbal.
And if I have the gift of prophecy and understand all the
sacred secrets and all knowledge,a and if I have all the
faith so as to move mountains, but do not have love, I am
nothing. And if I give all my belongings to feed others,
and if I hand over my body so that I may boast, but do not
have love, I do not benefit at all".
